## 👋 About Me

<img align="right" alt="Data Analytics GIF" width="320" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/qgQUggAC3Pfv687qPC/giphy.gif"/>

### 🌟 The Story
I'm not your typical analyst. I come from a **BBA + Data Analytics** background — which means before I ever write a query, I ask *"what does the business actually need?"*

While most people see rows and columns, I see **stories, patterns, and opportunities**. My journey into data analytics started with a simple curiosity: *why do some businesses thrive with the same data that others ignore?* The answer was always in how the data was **understood, questioned, and acted upon.**

### 🎯 What I Do
I bridge the gap between **raw data** and **real decisions**. Armed with SQL as my primary weapon and Power BI & Tableau as my storytelling tools, I build analyses that don't just sit in spreadsheets — they walk into boardrooms and drive strategy.
